Sex on the Beach

Description

Bartender's recipe for a classic fruity Sex on the Beach cocktail to enjoy by the water.

Ingredients

  • 1 fluid ounce Vodka of your choice
  • 1/2 fluid ounce melon liqueur
  • 1/2 fluid ounce raspberry liqueur
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounce pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounce cranbery juice

Steps

  1. Fill cocktail mixer with ice
  2. Combine vodka, melon liqueur, raspberry liqueur, pineapple juice, and cranberry juice in mixer
  3. Shake vigorously
  4. Strain into glass
