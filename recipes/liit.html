Long Island Iced Tea

Description

A great recipe for an all-time cocktail staple.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 fluid ounce vodka
  • 1/2 fluid ounce rum
  • 1/2 fluid ounce gin
  • 1/2 fluid ounce tequila
  • 1/2 fluid ounce triple sec
  • 1 fluid ounce sweet and sour mix
  • 1 fluid ounce cola
  • 1 lemon slice to garnish

Steps

  1. Fill cocktail shaker with ice.
  2. Pour vodka, rum, gin, tequila, triple sec, and sour mix over ice in shaker.
  3. Cover and shake.
  4. Pour cocktail into Collins or Hurricane glass.
  5. Top with a splash of cola.
  6. Garnish if desired.
